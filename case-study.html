From Enrollment Chaos to 87% Engagement

Simplifying Tech Education for 79k Students

Role Product Designer @ Meraki
Duration 5 Weeks
Key Metric 68.7% Retention

THE STORM: "I Missed Half My Classes Because of This App"

The challenge involved students from low-income backgrounds, often encountering technology for the first time. They faced significant hurdles with the existing platform:

  • Struggled to manually enroll in over 23 individual classes per course (like Python, English).
  • Found it difficult to distinguish revision classes from regular scheduled lessons.
  • Had trouble tracking complex schedules across different batches, frequently leading to missed Google Meet sessions.
Visual Placeholder:
Frustrated student + old cluttered interface vs. happy student + new UI

THE BREAKTHROUGHS: 3 Radical Simplifications

1

"Why Force 23 Clicks?" → One-Click Batch Enrollment

We drastically streamlined the process. A single batch signup now automatically enrolls students in all relevant classes and syncs their schedule via Google Calendar.

Result: Reduced enrollment complexity from dozens of clicks to just one.

Visual Placeholder:
GIF showing Before/After of the enrollment flow.
2

"Lost in Revision" → Dedicated Revision Hub

A new, dedicated section allows students to easily find and join revision classes from other batches on their own time, eliminating the need for teacher intervention.

Result: Empowered students with self-serve access to supplementary learning.

3

"Everything Looks the Same!" → Visual Course Roadmaps

The user interface was decluttered significantly. Irrelevant batches were hidden, and revision classes were clearly marked with distinct visual cues (like color-coding).

Result: Improved clarity and reduced cognitive load for navigating courses.

IMPACT: Scaling to 79k Students & Government Partnerships

The redesign delivered substantial, measurable improvements:

68.67% Retention Rate
87.5% Engagement Rate
79k+ Active Students

Retention skyrocketed compared to the industry average (~30-40% for edtech). This success directly contributed to the Haryana Government onboarding as a partner post-redesign (Dec 2021).

"Now I focus on learning, not button-clicking!" - Meraki Student

BEHIND THE SCENES: Designing for Shy, First-Time Tech Users

Understanding the unique user base was critical:

  • Overcoming Language Barriers: Conducted user interviews primarily in Hindi and other local languages with the help of volunteers to ensure genuine feedback.
  • Building Trust & Reducing Intimidation: Utilized low-fidelity paper prototypes during initial testing phases. This made users feel more comfortable providing honest feedback compared to polished digital mockups.
  • Stakeholder Alignment: Successfully convinced the engineering team of the value of auto-enrollment by demonstrating the significant time savings (estimated 20+ hours per student over the course duration).
